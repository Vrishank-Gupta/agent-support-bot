### What each change delivers

| Change | Problem solved | Cost impact |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| `system-prompt.md` | Contradictions, weak one-step rule, stray text, duplicate rules | −35% prompt tokens |
| `sessionState.ts` | Full state JSON sent every turn regardless of stage | −20–30% per turn |
| `trimConversationHistory.ts` | History grows unbounded on long sessions | Caps after turn 10 |
| `gpt-4o-mini` | gpt-5.2 is 40–80x over the 5 paisa budget | ~₹1.5–3 per query |
| `storage.ts` fix | Orphaned DB rows accumulate silently | Clean DB over time |
