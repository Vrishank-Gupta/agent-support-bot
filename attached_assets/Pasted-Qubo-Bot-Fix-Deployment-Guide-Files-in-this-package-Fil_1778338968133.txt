One-step enforcement — what changed in the prompt
The old prompt had soft wording ("give one step at a time") that the model could override.
The new prompt has three layers:
HARD OUTPUT RULE in PERSONALITY — explicit, capitalised, no-exceptions framing
OUTPUT CONSTRAINT at the top of Stage 6B — stage-specific reinforcement
SELF-CHECK BEFORE EVERY SEND — makes the model verify before responding
This matches how LLMs are reliably constrained: rule stated once as a hard constraint,
reinforced at the exact point of failure (Stage 6B), and verified via self-check.
